Discworld Characters Network

An interactive visualization of the characters, books, series, and genres of Discworld. Explore the rich connections in Terry Pratchett's universe.

Please rotate your device to landscape mode for the best experience.

How to Interact:

  • Zoom & Pan: Use your mouse wheel to zoom in and out. Click and drag the background to pan the view.
  • Node Details: Hover over any node (shape) to see more information in a tooltip.
  • Highlight Connections: Click on any node to highlight it and its direct connections.
  • Reset Highlight: Click on the highlighted node again, or anywhere on the empty background, to reset the view.
  • Legend Interaction: Click on a legend item (e.g., a character, series, or genre) to highlight all related nodes and their connections. Click again to reset.
  • Legend Tooltip: Hover over a legend item to see a brief description.
