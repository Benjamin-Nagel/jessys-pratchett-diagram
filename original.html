Navigation

  • **Pan:** Click and drag the background to move the entire diagram.
  • **Zoom:** Use your mouse scroll wheel to zoom in and out.
  • **Reset View:** Click on an empty area of the background to reset any active highlight and return to the default view.

Interaction

  • **Hover:** Hover over a book or character node to see its details in a tooltip.
  • **Click Nodes:** Click on a specific book or character node to highlight it and its direct connections (related books, characters, series, or genres).
  • **Click Legend Items:** Click on an item in the character, series, or genre legends to highlight all associated nodes and links in the diagram.
  • **Toggle Legends:** Use the `+`/`-` button next to legend titles to collapse or expand their content (feature to be implemented).
